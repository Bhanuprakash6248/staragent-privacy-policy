# Privacy Policy for StarAgent

**Last updated:** 19-03-2026

## 1. Introduction

StarAgent ("we," "our," or "us") is a CRM (Customer Relationship Management) mobile application designed for agents to manage their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using StarAgent, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you register, we collect:
- **Email address** (required)
- **Full name** (required)
- **Password** (stored in encrypted form)
- **Phone number** (optional)

### 2.2 Customer Data
As an agent, you may add and manage customer records in the app. This data includes:
- Customer names
- Phone numbers
- Policy details (e.g., policy name, proposal date, converted date, expiry date)
- EMI and payment-related information

You are responsible for ensuring you have consent to collect and store this customer data.

### 2.3 Payment & Subscription Information
When you subscribe via Google Play:
- **Google Play account** and **purchase token** are used to verify your subscription
- We do not store your credit card or payment details; payment is processed by Google.

### 2.4 Technical & Usage Data
We may collect:
- **Device information** (e.g., device type, operating system)
- **IP address** and **user-agent** (for security and session management)
- **App usage** (e.g., feedback submission)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|--------|-----------|
| **Account creation & login** | Email, name, password |
| **Password reset** | Email (to send OTP) |
| **CRM functionality** | Customer data you manage |
| **Subscription verification** | Payment token, product ID |
| **Support & feedback** | Your messages, feedback content |
| **Security & fraud prevention** | IP, device info, session tokens |

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- **Storage:** Data is stored on secure servers (cloud hosting providers).
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas.
- **Backend:** Hosted on Vercel.
- **Security:** Passwords are hashed, tokens are encrypted, and we use industry-standard security practices.
- **Retention:** We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion (see Section 7).

## 11. Account deletion and data deletion

You can request deletion of your StarAgent account and associated data at any time.

To delete your account, please email us at **support.staragent@gmail.com** from the email address used in your StarAgent account and mention “Account deletion request” in the subject.

After we verify your request, we will delete your account and associated data (account details and customer records) from our active systems within 30 days, except for any information we are required to retain for legal, accounting, or security reasons. Such retained data will be kept only as long as required by law.
